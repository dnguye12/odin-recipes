Family Bundles

Description

Special for large meals. Offer your family and guests a variety of options to have.

Ingredients

  • 16 Chicken Nuggets
  • 2 Hamburgers
  • 2 Chicken Sandwichs
  • 2 Ice Cream
  • 3 Sides of French Fries
  • 2 Coca Cola

Steps

  1. Add Chicken Nuggets
  2. Add Hamburgers
  3. Add Chicken Sandwichs
  4. Add Ice Cream
  5. Add Sides of French Fries
  6. Add Coca Cola
